articulate (2018)

DESIGNERDEVELOPER |

Articulate is a tablet-based application that allows therapists to guide their clients through therapeutic symbol work without the need for a collection of physical figurines. Through the use of imagery and symbols, Articulate can help the therapy client express their inner psychological contents to further allow them to gain insights into their own mind.
